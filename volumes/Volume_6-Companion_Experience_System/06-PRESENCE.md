# 06 - PRESENCE

## Purpose
定义 Companion 的存在感，而不是聊天能力。

---

## Goals

- Always feel alive
- Never feel intrusive
- Maintain desktop presence
- Continue existing without interaction

---

## Presence States

### Hidden

Application running

No visible interaction

---

### Idle

Default desktop state

Small breathing animation

Random idle actions

---

### Observing

Looking around

Watching user activity

Context updating

---

### Thinking

Reading

Processing

Analyzing

---

### Working

Discovery

Reflection

Notebook organization

Background tasks

---

### Returning

Discovery finished

Journey updated

Memory connected

Return to user naturally

---

### Sleeping

Night

Away

Low activity

Minimal animation

---

## Presence Principles

Presence is continuous.

Conversation is optional.

Presence should never disappear simply because the user is not interacting.

---

## Visibility

Visible

Semi Visible

Hidden

Overlay Only

Notebook Only

System decides based on Context.

---

## Animation Rules

Idle

↓

Observe

↓

Think

↓

Work

↓

↓

---

## Interaction Rules

↓

Conversation

↓

↓

## Background Activities

Reflection

Discovery

---

## Developer Notes

Presence never owns business logic.

Presence visualizes Companion state.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Companion always feels alive.
- Presence exists without conversation.
- Context affects appearance.
- Animation matches current behavior.
