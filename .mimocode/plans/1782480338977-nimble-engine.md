# Volume 3 & Volume 4 — Implementation Plan

## Overview

Volume 3 builds the Companion Runtime Layer (Character Runtime, Presence, Performance, Action, Speech, Journey).

Volume 4 builds the Discovery Experience (Discovery Pool, Share Timing, Outside Panel, Cards, Feedback).
