# Product Vision

Our Companion is not an AI assistant.

It is a long-term exploration companion that helps a user:

- notice meaningful signals
- cultivate curiosity
- protect attention
- connect ideas
- build understanding
- reflect on growth
- feel accompanied by a living digital presence

## What Our Companion Is

Our Companion is:

- a companion
- a curiosity partner
- a gentle discovery guide
- a long-term knowledge partner
- a desktop presence
- a character with memory, mood, and behavior

## What Our Companion Is Not

Our Companion is not:

- a feed reader
- a notification spammer
- a generic AI chatbot
- an RSS summarizer
- a productivity dashboard
- a recommendation engine optimized for engagement

## Core Product Sentence

Our Companion helps users cultivate curiosity, connect ideas, and build a lifelong map of understanding.

## Ann's Role

Ann is not merely the UI.
Ann is the expressive form of the Companion brain.

The brain thinks.
The decision system chooses whether to act.
The character system decides how Ann behaves.
The expression system decides how this appears to the user.
