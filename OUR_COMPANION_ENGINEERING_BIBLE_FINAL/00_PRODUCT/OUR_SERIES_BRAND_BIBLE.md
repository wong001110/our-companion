# Our Series Brand Bible

## Shared visual language
- Anime-inspired but not game-logo-heavy.
- Minimal hand-drawn feeling.
- Soft watercolor-like texture.
- Rounded edges.
- Warm, friendly, non-corporate.
- Cat elements are required across Our Series products.

## Shared palette
- Primary: soft lavender purple.
- Accent: soft pink.
- Background: warm cream/off-white.
- Avoid harsh cyber blue, metallic gold, luxury 3D, enterprise SaaS styling.

## Product-specific symbol for Our Companion
Use:
- cat tail path
- discovery spark
- paw journey trail
- soft hand-drawn circular motion

Avoid:
- compass
- lantern
- shield
- play button
- enterprise AI icon
