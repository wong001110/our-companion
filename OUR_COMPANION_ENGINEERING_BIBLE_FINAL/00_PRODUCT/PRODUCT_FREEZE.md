# Product Freeze

## One-line definition
Our Companion is a desktop exploration companion that discovers, remembers, learns, and explores alongside the user while assisting with digital tasks.

## Final positioning
Our Companion is:
- An exploration companion.
- A desktop pet-like character with behavior and presence.
- A discovery agent that finds interesting topics/resources.
- A memory/journey system that records what the user explored.
- A task assistant for safe browser/app actions.

Our Companion is not:
- A virtual girlfriend.
- A desktop secretary.
- A generic AI chat client.
- A pure productivity automation tool.
- A marketplace platform.

## Business / ecosystem decisions
- Free/open-source direction.
- No marketplace in v1.
- No public plugin system in v1.
- Belongs to Our Series.
- Cat element is mandatory in brand language.
- Our Series shared palette: soft lavender purple + soft pink.
